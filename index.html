Elif Norris is a Jill of all trades.

Over the past decade she has worked in an array of multidisciplinary fields in Berlin, New York and Los Angeles.

While her background lies in production design with a master's degree in set and costume design, Elif has managed a gallery, lead design teams in several theatre and film productions, been a stylist for companies like Levi‘s and Condé Nast, taught yoga while refining her public speaking skills and most recently made her debut in the web3 space as the co-creator of Otherhomes, an NFT project striving to help house the unhoused. She is also an active member of the Web3 Baddies, a community of womxn working toward a more inclusive space for all on the blockchain.

Elif loves people, bringing them together, forward and up. Communication, brand voice, organization and goal orientation are strengths that come naturally to her. She is bilingual in English and German and speaks Italian on a conversational level.

The web3 space is exhilarating and Elif trusts that the world is it’s oyster.
